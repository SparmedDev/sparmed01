About Us

Sparmed is a Danish company specialised in In Vitro Fertilisation and clean air solutions.

Description

Sparmed was founded in 2007 and launched the first product Oosafe® Filter in November 2007. Second product called Oosafe® Air Cleaner was launched in April 2009. Two months after, Oosafe® CUBE, Oosafe® Prefilter and Oosafe® MEA Tested Non-toxic IVF Laboratory Disinfectants followed air cleaner. We are happy to add Oosafe IVF Laboratory Outfit and Boomerang IVF Laboratory Quality Control System to our product range in June 2010.
